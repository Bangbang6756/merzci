Company Profile

Who We Are

CM & SONS FOOD PRODUCTS, INC. is a leading bread and food pasalubong producer in Bacolod City and the island of Negros. It maintains high standards in its manufacturing processes and is constantly updating and improving its product lines. Along with its constant improvements, it strives to deliver customer satisfaction by bringing the highest “Value for Money” to its clientele.

Corporate Vision

CM & SONS FOOD PRODUCTS, INC., thru its flagship brand Merzci Bread and Pastries, envisions itself to be the most preferred bakeshop, fast food, and food pasalubong establishment in the Philippine archipelago.
